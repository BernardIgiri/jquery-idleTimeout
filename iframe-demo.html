Jquery-idleTimeout - iFrame Demonstration - by JillElaine

GitHub Project Page - https://github.com/JillElaine/jquery-idleTimeout

This page demonstrates the 'bubbling' of activity events from an iframe within the same domain to the parent page.

Activity events that are detected are clicks, keypresses, and wheel scrolling.

To see the 'idle timeout' functionality, do not press any keys, click your mouse or scroll your mousewheel for 15 seconds.
